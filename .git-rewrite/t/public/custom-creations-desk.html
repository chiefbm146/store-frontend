Our Creative Services

Moon Tide Reconciliation offers custom Indigenous-inspired artwork that honors traditional designs while bringing your unique vision to life. Every piece is created with cultural respect, authenticity, and deep connection to Indigenous artistic traditions.

Custom Logos

Indigenous-inspired logos for organizations, businesses, and initiatives that honor cultural traditions while representing your unique identity.

Original Designs

Custom artwork for events, publications, merchandise, and spaces that incorporate Indigenous art forms and symbolism with contemporary applications.

Wood Carvings

Traditional cedar carvings, talking sticks, and ceremonial pieces crafted with respect for ancestral carving techniques and spiritual significance.

Beadwork & Crafts

Custom beaded pieces, regalia elements, and handcrafted items that carry cultural meaning and are created with traditional methods.

Educational Materials

Custom illustrations and designs for educational resources, curriculum materials, and publications about Indigenous culture and reconciliation.

Ceremonial Items

Sacred and ceremonial pieces created for specific purposes, protocols, and community needs with cultural guidance and approval.

Our Process

We work collaboratively with you to ensure your custom creation is authentic, meaningful, and perfectly suited to your needs.

1

Consultation

We discuss your vision, purpose, and how Indigenous artistic traditions can authentically represent your goals.

2

Cultural Guidance

We ensure all designs honor Indigenous protocols, symbolism, and cultural significance appropriately.

3

Design & Creation

Our artists develop your custom piece, incorporating your feedback throughout the creative process.

4

Delivery & Education

We deliver your piece along with information about its cultural elements and how to use or display it respectfully.
