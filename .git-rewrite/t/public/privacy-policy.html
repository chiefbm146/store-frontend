1. Introduction

Moon Tide Reconciliation ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website and payment platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide during checkout:

  • Name: For order fulfillment
  • Email: For order confirmation and communications
  • Phone Number: For delivery coordination
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe (we never see card details)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmation and shipping updates
  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Improve our services and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage & Security

Your personal information is stored in secure, encrypted databases. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest in Google Cloud Firestore
  • Role-based access controls
  • No card data stored on our servers (Stripe handles all payment data)

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill your order
  • Handle customer support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax records, dispute resolution)

Note: We are implementing automated deletion of non-essential data after 90 days. If you wish to have your data deleted sooner, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

  • Request Access: Email us to request a copy of your personal data
  • Request Deletion: Use our secure portal to submit a data deletion request
  • Withdraw Consent: Email us to opt-out of marketing communications

Response Time: We will respond to data access and deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Third Parties

We share your information only with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (PCI compliant)
  • Delivery Partners: Only with your name, phone, and address for shipping
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

8. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. Your privacy is respected.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
